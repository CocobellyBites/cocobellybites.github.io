Our Bites

Jamaican Patty A savory pastry traditionally filled with seasoned ground beef. Its golden color comes from curry and turmeric, which also give it a rich, distinct flavor. Today, Jamaican patties come with all kinds of fillings, from curry chicken to ackee, and even lobster.

The most popular way to eat a patty in Jamaica is tucked inside cocobread, soft and slightly sweet bread that turns the patty into a satisfying sandwich.

Get ready to bite into our homemade, flaky patties packed with love and spice.
